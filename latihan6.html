Choutokkyuu (超特急) also known as Bullet Train is currently a nine-member Japanese boy group under EBiDAN affiliated with STARDUST PROMOTION. Formed on December 25, 2011 and made their CD Debut on June 10, 2012 with single "TRAIN". The group concept is main dancer and back vocal, where the dancers are dancing in the front while the vocalist is singing in the back. Choutokkyuu doesn't have a permanent center. The center of the group will change for each song. Each member has a color and a specific role. They also represent the cars of a train with their numbers. They are diverse in style, but mostly known for their 'lame and cool ' (dasai-kakkoi) style. Choutokkyuu is well known with their high entertainment skills and the live performance that is full of unity with their fans (8gousha). In 2018 they won their first award on MAMA2018 in Japan.

Trivia

  • Car (Train) number 8 is fans, the fandom name is 8号車 (Hachigousha). The image colour is Pink.
  • Car (Train) number 9 is staff and related parties, 9号車 (Kyuugousha). The image colour is Navy Blue. Their senior, Kitagawa Keiko, is also 9号車. Her image colour is Rainbow.
  • Bullet Train is known as a brother and rival group of the popular rock band DISH// (graduated from EBiDAN in August 2022). Both of the groups were formed on December 25, 2011 and made their CD Debut on the same day (June 10, 2012). They did a lot of activities such as stages and variety shows together in their early era.
